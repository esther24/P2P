Why is Sign Language
Important?

Language is the backbone of our entire Society. Without it, communication of any kind of knowledge won't be possible. To imagine a world without Language is not possible. To have gaps in communication just because of a physical Impairment is unjust even though there are sign languages available people do not tend to use them. Thus to make lives of millions of physically impaired people it is important to learn and apply it.

How do we help?

Bridging Communication Gaps.

Our platform is built to bridge the existing communication gaps while conversing with physically imapired people.

Learning and applying what is learnt is a fun way to remember common signs.
